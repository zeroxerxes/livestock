Welcome To Ostrich Poultry Farmers Exporters

In serenity, calmness and tranquillity lies Ostrich Poultry Farmers Exporters, 5km from civilization yet so free from the daily rush of life. Based in Gardenvale, Kliprivier we are central to most areas in Bloemfontein and Vereeniging. We cater for all your sheep, goat and cattle requirements. We strive to offer livestock that would suit your budget and we will go the extra mile to source it for you should we not have it in stock. We also offer extra services and facilities to make your experience a memorable one. Services include: slaughter, skinning, cold storage, slicing, packing, labelling and delivering to your door. Getting your work done for you was never this easy, visit our “about us” page for full list of services and feel free to call or email us and we will respond to your query in no time.
Situated on Ptn 153 of the Farm, Ostrich Poultry Farmers Exporters. We are buyers and sellers of livestock providing you with the best service and quality, ensuring that we put a smile on your face while saving you a dent in your pocket. We stock sheep, goats and cattle to suite your budget and needs. All slaughtering is done in a highly professional and hygienic abattoir style facility which conforms to standards as set by the South African Abattoirs Association. For extra satisfaction and peace of mind for our clients, we are also SANHA approved. For your convenience we offer service such as slaughtering, slicing, packaging and delivery to most areas in Bloemfontein.
